The work

Leading architects. One trusted builder.

The best residential architecture only reaches its potential in the hands of a builder who understands it. For decades, Square One has been that builder — the quiet partner behind award-winning, widely published homes designed by some of the country’s most respected practices. We don’t compete with the design. We realize it.

That means reading a detail the way the architect intended, holding the tolerances that make restraint possible, and bringing a level head to the field conditions every ambitious project encounters. It also means an open, communicative process — so the architect, the client, and the builder stay aligned from preconstruction through final punch.

Preconstruction is where it’s won

The most valuable work happens before a wall goes up. Engaging early, we partner on detailed estimating, value engineering, constructability review, and phasing — so an ambitious design withstands contact with reality and the budget. It is the discipline that keeps a vision intact when the bids come in, and the reason architects bring us in at the start.

Built with leading practices

We’ve built homes designed by David Jameson, FAIA; Tom Kundig of Olson Kundig; Todd Webb of Dirk Denison Architects; MODE4 Architecture; and others — work recognized with national AIA honors, International Architecture Awards, and features in Dwell, Interior Design, and Architectural Record. See the portfolio and the full record on the Recognition page.
