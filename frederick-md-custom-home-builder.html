Building in Frederick

The caliber of work the city deserves.

Frederick County spans a remarkable range — the historic streets and protected districts of Downtown Frederick and Baker Park, the established character of Braddock Heights and Middletown, and the estate properties of Ijamsville, Urbana, New Market, and Lake Linganore. Each calls for a builder who can move fluidly between sensitive historic work and ambitious new construction, and who treats every site, old or new, with the same rigor.

That is the standard we bring to Frederick: the discipline, craft, and discretion behind award-winning homes in DC, Bethesda, and across the region — now intentionally focused closer to home.
