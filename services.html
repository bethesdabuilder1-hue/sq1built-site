What we do

Services

From the first conversation with your architect to the final detail — and the care of the home for years after — we build to one standard: exceptional. Here's how we work.

01 — Preconstruction

Protecting the design — and the budget.

The most valuable work happens before a wall goes up. Engaging early, we partner with you and your architect on value engineering, detailed estimating, constructability, phasing, and cost management — so an ambitious design withstands contact with reality. It's the discipline that keeps your vision intact when the bids come in, and the service architects most want in a builder.

More on architect-led construction →

02 — New Construction

Bespoke homes, built without compromise.

We specialize in one-of-a-kind residences and the details others avoid — hillside sites, deep basements, structural glass walls, intricate pools, dramatic cantilevers. Our open, communicative process keeps you informed at every stage, so the home you imagined with your architect is the home that gets built — down to the execution tolerances demanded by modern work: flush reveals, seamless millwork and matched wood veneers, and fully integrated lighting and AV.

More on custom homes in DC, MD & VA →

03 — Renovation

Transformations that honor the architecture.

From a single refined space to a full gut renovation or addition, we reimagine homes with the same rigor we bring to new builds. Several of our most-awarded projects are renovations — proof that the right builder can elevate an existing home into something extraordinary.

More on renovations →

04 — Condominiums

Jewel-box precision in the city.

High-end condominium build-outs and unit combinations demand millimeter precision and seamless coordination within a building's constraints. We deliver gallery-calm interiors — custom millwork, integrated lighting and glass, flawless finishes — that live far larger than their footprint.

More on condominium renovations →

05 — Square One Care

The relationship doesn't end at move-in.

Most builders hand over the keys and disappear. We stay. Square One Care is an ongoing membership for the homes we've built — and for other distinctive homes that deserve a builder who truly understands them. It's an avenue few high-end builders offer, and a reason our clients stay with us for decades.
