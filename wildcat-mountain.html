Wildcat Mountain

David Jameson, FAIA · Built by PureForm Builders
The Project

A hilltop home of stone, glass, and Blue Ridge views.

Set into a hilltop in The Plains, Wildcat Mountain is defined by four major stone walls that blend into the landscape. Between them, open-air patios and glass-walled rooms frame spectacular views in every direction, with an indoor/outdoor pavilion opening toward the Blue Ridge Mountains.

Inside, 25-foot ceilings soar above curved stone walls unified by a continuous floor — a home that captures and reflects the raw beauty of its surroundings.
